## Stephanie Kasulka

Hi there! I’m Stephanie, a senior-year student at Lehigh University majoring in journalism and minoring in film studies. I am passionate about local journalism and aim to tell informative, impactful, and inclusive stories. My previous work appears in Hudson Valley magazine, a publication native to my home in Westchester, New York, as well as the lifestyle section of Lehigh’s school newspaper, the Brown and White, and [Parents.com](https://www.parents.com/stephanie-kasulka-8668068).
